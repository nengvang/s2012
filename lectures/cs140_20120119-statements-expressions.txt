Statements and Expressions:

	Assignment statement
		<lhs> = <rhs>
		m = 3
		
		Left hand side must be assignable/mutable
		3 = m	# invalid
		
		Right hand side must be an expression
	
	Expression is a combination of operators and operands
	that evaluates to a single value*.
		Examples:
			3, 'Hello, World!', 2.818, True
			2 + 2, 4 * 8, 32 / 16, 10 - 7
			4 > 2, 2 > 4, x >= 10, 3 <= 11, 8 != 8, 5 == 10
			4 > 2 or 2 > 4, (2 + 3) * 10 == 50
