QB Passer Rating

Algorithm:
	1. Request five statistical inputs
		pass completions
		pass attempts
		total passing yards
		touchdowns
		interceptions
	2. Calculate intermediate values
		C = (Comp/Att - 0.3) * 5 or ((Comp/Att * 100) - 30) / 20
		Y = (Yds/Att - 3) * 0.25 or (Yds/Att - 3) / 4
		T = (TD/Att) * 20
		I  = 2.375 - (Int/Att * 25)
	3. Combine intermediates to get passer rating
		rating = (C + Y + T + I) / 6 * 100
